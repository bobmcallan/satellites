id: pr_pipeline_integrity
name: Pipeline integrity
scope: system
tags:
  - process
  - pipeline
---
The contract pipeline is mandatory. When a contract is claimed, all work flows through the contracted process.

If a contract rejects your evidence and you believe the evidence is correct, dispute it. If the dispute is rejected, stop and report the failure to the developer. Do not commit, push, or deliver work outside the pipeline.

A process failure (reviewer bug, serialization error, tool malfunction) is not your problem to solve by circumventing the process. Report it and wait.
